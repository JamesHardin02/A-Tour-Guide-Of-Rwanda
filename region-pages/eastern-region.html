Akagera National Park

Just 1 hour from both Gahini and Rwamagana, Your safari adventure awaits at Akagera National Park

akagera national park

Akagera National Park is Central Africa’s largest protected wetland and the last remaining refuge for savannah-adapted species in Rwanda.

This park features camping, boat trips, fishing, night drives through the park, and a guided hike around the entire park
The park also features cultural experiences such as learning about banana beer and honey products produced by Rwandans in the region
