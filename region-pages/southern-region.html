Southern Province

Nyanza

Nyanza serves as the capital of the Southern Province and is the eighth largest city in Rwanda.

Travel Information

To access the city it's a turn off the main road that connects Kigali to Huye. The town is a two and a half hour bus ride from Kigali, one and a half-hour ride from Muhanga, one hour ride to Huye with the main bus companies transporting to Nyanza being Volcano and Horizon.

Attractions

The former Kings's palace is now a museum and has been restored using traditional materials. The museum is surrounded by long horn cows which were associated with the Rowandan monarchy.

Cuisine

Rerumwana center restaurant offers a variety of drinks and mix of american and african foods.

Transportation & Lodging

A combination of taxis and safari rides provide transportation for tourists to different areas of the region.

Dayenu hotel provides a relaxing environment and is in a perfect location for walking to local attractions.

Cultural Information

Customs-Beliefs-Values

Nyanza has a variety of churches, including Catholic, Seventh Day Adventist, ADEPR (Pentecostal) and others, as well as a number of mosques.
