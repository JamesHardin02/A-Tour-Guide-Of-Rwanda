Capital: Kibuye

Kibuye, which is right on the shores of Lake Kivu, is the perfect spot for a relaxing and adventurous retreat.

Travel Information

Attractions

Kivu Tours

Take in the sights and explore some trails by a guided tour of Lake Kivu with Kivu Tours

Iliza Houseboat

Or if it suites your fancy, take a personal cruise out on Lake Kivu on the cozy Iliza Houseboat

Museum of Environment

Learn of how the Rwandan Gov. preserves and nourishes their beautiful country at the Meusum of Environment

Cuisine

Sailors Restaurant and Cafe

Enjoy an fine meal and a beautiful view at Sailors Restaurant and Cafe.

Transportation & Lodging

The serene Cormaran Lodge offers most hospitable lodging with the most breathtaking views of Lake Kivu.
The VIP room offers a view of Nyiragongo Volcano across the lake in neighboring Congo.

Mount Karisimbi

Mission Africa Safaris

Offers extensive and adventurous tours through the Western country side of Rwanda

Mission Africa Safaris offers guided hikes and gorilla treks up Mount Karisimbi
which is right along the border with the Democratic Republic of Congo
Mission Africa Safaris also offers many choices of lodging detailed on their site according to which tour you wish to embark on

Nyungwe Forest National Park

Nyungwe Forest National Park offers guided hikes and chimpanzee treks down the trails of the park. Explore the beautiful jungle and enjoy the adorable and friendly wildlife.
Nyungwe Forest National Park's website also links to other tour agencies that offer wildlife tours and accomodations
