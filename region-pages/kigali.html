Major City: Gasabo

Gasabo is the largest district in the capital city of Kigali.

Travel Information

Amahoro National Stadium

Amahoro National Stadium is the largest sports stadium in Rwanda and is home to 2 major football teams.

Cuisine

Inka Steakhouse is an upscale steakhouse serving some of the best food and cocktails in Gasabo.



Transportation & Lodging

Grand Legacy Hotel is a 3-star hotel with plenty of Amenities to keep you feeling relaxed and refreshed. It is also within walking distance of Amahoro Stadium.
