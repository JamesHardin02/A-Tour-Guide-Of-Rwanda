Northern Region

Major City

Byumba, located in the Gicumbi district is the capital of the Northern Province of Rwanda. Byumba is home to the SOS Childrens Village, which provides education and health care the the local children.

Cuisine

Maya's Restaurant is located in the city of Ruhengeri, serving traditional Rwandan food with great service welcoming atmoshere!

Transportation & Lodging

Daily coach services run all throughout the Northern Province on paved major roads. There are no railways in Rwanda, however shared taxis are very popular among Rwandans.
There are plenty of lodges to stay at in the Northern province, most famously the Virunga Lodge hotel in Burera. Located between Lakes Ruhondo and Burera, and near the Virunga mountains, this lodge offers amazing scenery.

Cultural Information

Customs-Beliefs-Values

Although Christianity makes up 44% of Rwandas religious beliefs several other forms of religion have made their way into the country. Jehovah's witness is a small one making up about 0.7%, Kingdom Halls can be found all throughout the Northern Province.

Language

Kinyarwanda is a language spoken by several African countries, with 93% of Rwandans speaking this language it is the official language of Rwanda. Other official languages include French, English and Swahili.

Art

Inshuti Arts and Culture Center showcases some of the best art Rowanda has to offer and is located in the city of Ruhengeri.
