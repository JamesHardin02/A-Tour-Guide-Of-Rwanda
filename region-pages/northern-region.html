Northern Region

Major Cities

The Northern Province of Rwanda is comprised of 5 Major districts: Burera, Gakenke, Gicumbi, Rulindo and Musanze.
Musanze is the capital of the Northern Province and is home of the Volcanoes National Park, coupled with its
large population of mountain gorillas this makes it a very popular tourist destination.

Travel Information

Attractions

  • Volcanoes Nationl Park - Musanze.
  • Virunga Lodge hotel - Burera.
  • Karisoke Research Center - Musanze.
  • Rwanda Liberation Museum - Gicumbi.

Cuisine

Brochette, a skewered meat, is very popular around lake Ruhondo where fish is plentiful. The fish is skewered with vegetables and roasted over a fire until chared black

Transportation & Lodging

Daily coach services run all throughout the Northern Province on paved major roads. There are no railways in Rwanda, however shared taxis are very popular among Rwandans.
There are plenty of lodges to stay at in the Northern province, most famously the Virunga Lodge hotel in Burera. Located between Lakes Ruhondo and Burera, and near the Virunga mountains, this lodge offers amazing scenery.
